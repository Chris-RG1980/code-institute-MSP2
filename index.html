Instructions

Objective: Match pairs of letters hidden in the grid to earn points and test your memory!

Number of Players: 1

Gameplay:

  1. Click on any tile to reveal the character underneath it. You can only click on two tiles at a time.
  2. If the two tiles you've clicked on reveal the same character, it's a match!
  3. You'll earn one point for every successful match.
  4. If the two tiles you've clicked on do not reveal the same character, they will flip back face-down and one point will be added to the no match box.
  5. Keep playing and matching until all pairs of characters have been revealed.

How to win:

If the Match score is higher than the no match score then you have won.
