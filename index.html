The Matching Games

Match & Win!!!

Instructions

Objective: Match pairs of images hidden in the grid to earn points and test your memory!

Number of Players: 1

Gameplay:

  1. Select a game to play below.
  2. Click on any tile to reveal the character beneath it. You can only click on two tiles at a time.
  3. If the two tiles you've clicked on reveal the same character, it's a match!
  4. You'll earn one point for every successful match.
  5. If the two tiles you've clicked on do not reveal the same character, they will flip back face-down.
  6. Keep playing and matching until all pairs of characters have been revealed.
  7. If you would like to reset the game at any time simply press the reset button at the bottom of the game.

How to win:

Match all characters in the game and you have won.

Play the game again and try to beat your previous time.
